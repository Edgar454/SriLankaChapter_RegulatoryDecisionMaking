2. It has come to the notice of the Tea Board that certain
manufacturers/exporters who export bulk tea in chests or paper sacks are
using the Lion Logo on such bulk packaging without approval.

3. Please note that the use of the Lion Logo is permitted only on
consumer packs (ie. packets, bags, caddies etc). The use of the Lion Logs
on any form of packaging containing bulk tea is not allowed and exporters
are hereby requested to refrain from using the Logo on such packaging. The
Director General, Customs is being advised not to permit any shipments of
bulk teas with the Lion Logo on or after 1st November, 1993.
