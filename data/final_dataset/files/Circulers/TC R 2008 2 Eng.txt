COSTS OF TRANSPORTING GREEN TEA LEAF
Taking into consideration the prevailing price of Rs.110 per liter of diesel fuel, the
transport rates in my circular letter dated 12.03.2008 bearing reference TC/R/2008/1
have been revised as follows.
(1) For Registered Tea Manufacturers.

On the basis of transporting a maximum of 750 kgs. of green tea leaf per trip;

1. Upto 5 km - Rs. 3.00 ’
2: Between 6 and 15kms - Rs. 3.30
a Between 16 and 25 km - Rs. 3.60

Only a maximum of Rs. 3.60 must be charged for distances beyond 25kms.
Transporting green tea leaf for such distances is discouraged as it may cause post
harvest damage.
Registered tea manufacturers are strongly advised to adopt suitable methods to
prevent post harvest damage to green tea leaf while transporting.

(2) For Licensed Green Tea Leaf Dealers.

On the basis of transporting a maximum of 1250 kgs. of green tea leaf per trip;

1. Up to 5 kms - Rs.1.85

Pm From 6 to 15 kms - Rs.2.00

3. From 15 to 25 kms - RS.2.20

Only a maximum of Rs. 2.20 must be charged for distances beyond 25kms. «
Transporting green tea leaf for such distances is discouraged as it may cause post

harvest damage.

Licensed Green Tea Leaf Dealers are strongly advised to adopt suitable methods
to prevent post harvest damage to green tea leaf wife transporting.

This circular letter is effective from 1* June, 2008.

This circular letter cancels the circular letter No. TC/R/2008/1 dated 12.03.2008.
